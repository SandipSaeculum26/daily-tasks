How To Play

According to the applicable legislation, you are entitled to a replacement car for a maximum period established by ASF Norm no. 20/2017, updated by ASF Norm no. 18/2022, Article 25, which states:

Art. 25: (1) In the event that, as a result of a risk covered by the RCA insurance contract, the vehicle of the injured party is damaged, the RCA insurer shall compensate for the loss of use of the damaged vehicle during the repairs period, in accordance with the applicable legal provisions.

1. Personal documents

  • • Identity Card (ID) / Passport – copy
  • • Driving License – copy

2. Documents related to the accident and compensation

  • • Accident report issued by the Police
  • • Amicable accident statement, completed and signed by both parties involved
  • • Proof of damage file registration at the RCA insurer of the at-fault driver
  • • Damage assessment report issued by the insurer
  • • Payment acceptance from the RCA insurer to cover the loss of use of the damaged vehicle

3. Documents related to the damaged vehicle

  • • Vehicle Registration Certificate (logbook) – copy
  • • RCA Insurance Policy of the at-fault driver – copy
